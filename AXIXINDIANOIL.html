Features & Benefits of IndianOil Axis Bank Credit Card

Axis Bank Indian Oil credit card benefits and features are mentioned below:

Welcome Benefits

  • Indian Oil Axis Bank credit card benefits include 100% cashback (up to Rs. 250) on transactions across all fuel stations done within 30 days of card issuance.

Accelerated Reward Points

  • Get 20 reward points on spending every Rs. 400 at IOCL fuel outlets in India. 
  • Get 5 reward points on spending every Rs. 100 on online shopping. 
  • The transaction value should be between Rs. 100 and Rs. 5,000 per month to avail of these benefits. 
  • 1 Edge Loylty Reward Point on spending Rs. 1,000. 

Fuel Surcharge Waiver

  • 1% fuel surcharge waiver on fuel purchase at all fuel stations in India.
  • The transaction value should be between Rs. 200 and Rs. 5,000 per month to avail of these benefits.
  • Maximum benefit is limited to Rs. 50 per payment cycle.

Discounts

  • Instant discount of up to 10% on booking movie tickets from BookMyShow app or website.

Annual Fee Waiver

  • Spend above Rs. 50,000 in a year and get annual fee completely waived off.

Dining Benefits

  • Up to 20% discount on dining at partner restaurants in India.

IndianOil Axis Bank Credit Card Fees & Charges

Following are the Axis Bank Indian Oil credit card charges:

  • Joining fee: Rs. 500
  • Annual fee: Rs. 500
  • Card replacement fee: Rs. 100.
  • Cash payment fee: Rs. 100.
  • Finance charges for retail purchases and cash: 3.4% per month (49.36% per annum).
  • Cash withdrawal fee: 3.4% of the cash amount (minimum Rs. 500).
  • Overdue penalty/ late payment fee: Up to Rs. 500, depends on the total payment due.
  • Over limit penalty: 2.5% of the over limit amount (minimum Rs. 500).
  • Cheque return/ dishonor fee/ auto-debit reversal: 2% of the payment amount (minimum Rs. 450).
  • Foreign currency transaction fee: 3.5% of the total transaction value.

IndianOil Axis Bank Credit Card Eligibility

The detailed eligibility criteria of Axis Bank IndianOil credit card are mentioned below:

  • Age of the primary cardholder should be between 18 and 70 years.
  • Age of the add-on cardholder should be above 15 years.
  • Applicants can either be an Indian resident or a non-resident Indian (NRI).
  • To be eligible for this card, the applicant should be a salaried employee with a minimum monthly income of at least Rs. 20,000. 

IndianOil Axis Bank Credit Card Document Required

The following documents are required to apply for Indian Oil Axis credit card:

  • PAN Card copy/ Form 60
  • Recent coloured photograph
  • Income proof: Latest Salary Slip/ Form 16/ ITR copy.
  • Residence proof: Passport/ Ration Card/ Landline Telephone Bill/  Electricity Bill.
  • Identity proof: Passport/ Aadhaar Card/ Driving License/ PAN Card.

You can apply for IOCL credit card Axis Bank by following the steps mentioned below:
