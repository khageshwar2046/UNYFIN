Benefits and Features of SBI Card PRIME

  • Welcome Gift 

As a privilege customer of SBI Card PRIME, you get welcome e-voucher worth  3,000. The gift voucher can be used with any of the partner brands: Yatra.com, Hush Puppies/ Bata, Marks & Spencer, Pantaloons and Shoppers Stop.

  • Terms of Use
  • After paying the annual fees for the card, an SMS is sent to you within 15 days to share the choice for e-voucher options.
  • You can choose the e-vouchers according to your needs and request for the same. As you make the request, a voucher code will be sent to you on your registered mobile number or e-mail ID within 5 days.
  • You can use the voucher at partner brands such as Bata/ Hush Puppies, Marks, and Spencer, Pantaloons and Shoppers Stop, e-Gift Voucher by showing the voucher code at the respective retail store.
  • As for Yatra and Shoppers Stop, the code can be used instantly online while making a purchase.
  • PRIME Rewards
  • With SBI Card PRIME you can earn a host of rewards with utility bills payments and everyday purchases made online or at a retail store. 4 Reward Points fetch you  1 and thus you can make additional savings with each such purchase with your SBI Card PRIME. You can also pay out your credit card bills with accumulated Reward Points.
  • Earn Rewards as follows:
  • Earn 20 rewards points per 100 spent on standing instruction for Utility Bills
  • Another 10 Reward Points per 100 spent on dining, grocery purchase, shopping at a departmental store and movie tickets booking.
  • For all other spends, you add 2 Reward Points per 100 to your account.
  • Fuel Surcharge Waiver:
  • Enjoy 1% Fuel Surcharge Waiver across all petrol pumps pan India, subject to transactions ranging from 500- 4000 (Exclusive of service tax applicable) by SBI Card PRIME. A maximum of 250 waiver can be enjoyed by a cardholder each month.
  • Milestone Benefits
  • SBI Card extends additional milestone benefits to celebrate the valuable patronage with their customers.
  • Enjoy Pizza Hut e-Voucher worth  1,000 on spends of  50,000 in a calendar quarter
  • Reversal of Renewal Fee worth 2,999 on annual spends of  3 Lakh
  • E-Gift Voucher worth  7,000 from Yatra.com/ Pantaloons on annual spends of  5 Lakhs
  • Pizza Hut e-Vouchers are sent within 15 days of achieving spends of  50,000 to the registered mobile number of the cardholder.
  • As for Yatra.com/ Pantaloons voucher, an SMS is sent on the registered mobile number within 10 days of achieving spends of  5 Lakh. You can choose the e-Gift Voucher according to your preference.
  • Trident Privilege Membership
  • With SBI Card PRIME, primary cardholders get complimentary Trident Privilege Red Tier Membership. As you opt for the membership, avail 1,000 Welcome Points on registration.
  • Earn 1,500 Bonus Points on first stay; and additional  1,000 credit on the extended night stay. Promo code is SBITH
  • You can instantly save 10% on bookings through the hotel website.
  • Avail 10 points per 100 spent (excluding taxes) during your stay.
  • Club Vistara Membership
  • Complimentary Club Vistara Silver membership brings along 1 complimentary Lounge Access Voucher and 1 Upgrade Voucher.
  • 9 Club Vistara Points for every  100 spent on Vistara flights
  • The offer is only for primary cardholders.
  • Lounge Access
  • With SBI Card PRIME you can enjoy 4 visits to international priority pass lounges as a complimentary offer, subject to 2 maximum visits per quarter.
  • As for Domestic VISA/ Mastercard Lounges in India, you can enjoy 8 complimentary visits per year; maximum of 2 visits per quarter.
  • Birthday Benefit
  • 20 Reward Points per  100 spent on cardholder?s birthday
  • Mastercard/Visa Privileges
  • 4 complimentary rounds on Green Fees per year
  • Every month get 1 complimentary golf lesson at select courses in India
  • Access to exclusive cardholder benefits across 900 luxury hotels globally
  • Save up to 35% on Avis Car Rentals and 10% on Hertz car rental
  • Contactless Advantage
  • With ContactLess Advantage you can transact with your SBI Card without physically handing over the card. All you need to do is wave your card at a secure reader to transact. The transactions are fast & convenient and secure to process. The card is powered by the Visa payWave feature.
  • Global Acceptance
  • Your SBI Card PRIME is accepted at over 24 million outlets across the globe, inclusive of 3,25,000 Indian outlets.
  • Fraud Liability Cover
  • Exclusively for SBI Card PRIME holders, avail complimentary insurance cover against accident and card fraud.
  • 50 lakh Air Accident Liability Cover
  • 1 lakh
  • Credit card Fraud Liability Cover
  • Report card loss at SBI Card helpline: 1860 180 1290 or 39 02 02 02 (prefix local STD code). Report the loss within 2 -7 days.
  • Emergency Card Replacement
  • With SBI Card PRIME you get a privilege to replace your card anywhere in the world. A 24x7 helpline supports customers in case of emergency, and thus you can conveniently request card replacement even while travelling abroad.
  • Add-on Cards
  • With SBI Card PRIME, you can request add-on cards for 2 members above 18 years of age of your family.
  • Cash on the Go
  • You can conveniently withdraw cash from SBI Card PRIME from over 1 million Visa ATMs worldwide
  • Safe and Secure
  • SBI Card PRIME is a chip and pin enabled card that extends enhanced security against counterfeiting and skimming.
  • Easy Bill Pay Facility
  • All cardholders can conveniently pay utility bills for electricity, telephone, mobile, etc using the Easy Bill Pay facility.
  • Flexipay
  • Flexipay on SBI Card PRIME offers you the flexibility to convert spends of  2,500 or more into easy monthly instalments. Within 30 days of the purchase, you can request for Flexipay by logging into the website.
  • Balance Transfer on EMI
  • Balance Transfer facility empowers you to transfer the balance of your other credit cards to SBI Card PRIME at a lower rate of interest and affordable EMIs.

Fees and Charges

  • Annual Fee:  2,999 (one time)
  • Renewal Fee:  2,999 (per annum) from second year onwards; Renewal Fee is reversible on annual spends of 3 Lakh
  • Add-on Fee: Nil
  • Extended Credit:  Interest-free credit period: 20-50 days *previous month's outstanding must be nil to enjoy interest-free credit period
  • Finance charges: From transaction date it may cost up to 3.35% per month (i.e,  40.2% p.a)
  • Minimum Amount Due: 5% of Total Due (Min.  200 + applicable taxes + EMI ( if any) + Over Limit Amount (if any)
  • Limit on Cash Advance: Up to 80% of the approved credit limit, with a maximum of  15,000/ day
  • Free credit period: Nil
  • Cash Advance Fee: 2.5% of transaction amount ( minimum  300)
  • International ATMs: 3% of transaction amount, (minimum:  300)
  • Cash payment fee and Cheque pickup:  100 for each
  • Payment dishonor fee: 2% (minimum:  450)
  • Statement retrieval:  100 per statement *applicable on statments olders than 2 months
  • Cheque Fee:  100 (Payments made via cheque up to  10,000)
  • Late payment:
  • NIL for Total Amount due from 0 - 200;
  •  100 for Total Amount due greater than  200 & up to  500;
  •  400 for Total Amount due greater than  500 & up to  1000;
  •  600 for Total Amount due greater than  1000 & up to  10,000;
  •  800 for Total Amount due greater than  10,000 & up to  25,000;
  •  950 for Total Amount due greater than  25,000
  • Over Limit: 2.5% of over limit amount, subject to a minimum of  500
  • Card replacement:  100
  • Emergency card replacement, when abroad: Actual cost (subject to a minimum of $175)
  • Foreign currency transaction Conversion mark up: 3.5%
  • Dynamic Currency Conversion: Mark up Fee: 3.50%
  • Rewards redemption fee:  99
  • Priority Pass Lounge Charges: After exhausting the complimentary visits, all Airport Lounge visits within India and outside India will be charged with a usage fee of $27 per visit + applicable taxes. All guests visiting the Airport Lounge will be charged a standard usage fee of $27 per visit + applicable taxes.
  • Surcharges:  Railway tickets through counters:  30 + 2.5% of the transaction amount Railway tickets through irctc.co.in: 1.8% of transaction amount + service charge
  • At petrol pumps: 1% fuel surcharge waiver for each transaction between  500 and  4,000 (exclusive of taxes). Maximum surcharge waiver:  250 per statement cycle.
  • Payments of Customs duty: 2.25% of transaction amount (subject to a minimum of  75)
