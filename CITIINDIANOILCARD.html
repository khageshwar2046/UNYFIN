IndianOil Citi Credit Card Fees & Charges

Following are the Citibank IndianOil credit card charges in detail including the Citibank IndianOil credit card annual fee:

  • Joining fee: Nil.
  • Annual fee: Rs. 1000 + taxes; waived off on annual spends of Rs. 30,000 or more.
  • Foreign currency transactions mark-up: 3.5%.
  • Cash advance fees: 2.5% on the withdrawn billed amount, subject to a minimum of Rs. 500.
  • Late payment charges: Nil to Rs. 1,300, depending on the statement balance amount.
  • Cash deposit at Citi branches charges: Rs. 250 per deposit.
  • Over credit limit charge: 2.5% of the amount over the credit limit + fees, charges and taxes, subject to a minimum of Rs. 500. 
  • Payment return charges (Cheque/ ECS/ ACH/ SI bounce charge): Rs. 500 per returned payment. 
  • Fuel transaction charge: 1% + GST. This charge is reversed at authorized IndianOil Corporation outlets for transactions above Rs. 10.
  • Railway ticket booking surcharge: 1.8% of the transaction value.

Citibank IndianOil card offers the following reward points benefits:

  • Get activation Citibank IndianOil credit card benefits of 250 Turbo Points on your first transaction within 30 days of card issuance.
  • Earn 4 Turbo points on spending every Rs. 150

IndianOil Citi Credit Card Document Required

Following are the documents requirements for salaried and self-employed applicants for the Citi Bank Indian Oil credit card:

Documents Requirement for Salaried

  • Proof of Identity & Address: Valid Passport issued by the Government of India, Valid Driving License, Aadhaar Card, E-aadhaar, Voter's ID Card or Job Card issued by NREGA signed by an officer of the State Government.
  • Income Proof: Latest two salary slips

Documents Requirement for Self-employed

  • Proof of Identity & Address: Valid Passport issued by the Government of India, Valid Driving License, Aadhaar Card, E-aadhaar, Voter's ID Card or Job Card issued by NREGA signed by an officer of the State Government.
  • Income Proof: Latest year’s ITR with audited financials and computation of income.

IndianOil Citi Credit Card Eligibility

Below is the eligibility criteria required to be fulfilled for Citi IndianOil card:

  • You must have a CIBIL score of 700 or above.
  • You must be a salaried, self-employed professional or a businessman.
  • The minimum income requirement to get a Citibank credit card is Rs. 25,000 per month.

You can apply for Citibank Indian Oil credit card through the official website of the bank or its mobile app or by visiting the nearby Citibank branch. You can also apply for the card through MyMoneyMantra by following the steps mentioned below:

  • Click on the ‘Credit Cards’ option on the homepage of our website.
  • Now enter all the required details, including your name, mobile number, email id, employment information, income details, and so on.
  • Verify your mobile number by entering the OTP received on your number.
  • You will receive a call from our credit card professional soon to help you complete the credit card application process.
