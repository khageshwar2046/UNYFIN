Citibank Cash Back Credit Card Fees & Charges

Following are the fees and charges associated with the Citi Cashback card:

  • Joining fee: Rs. 500
  • Annual fee: Rs. 500
  • Interest rate: Ranges between 37.20% and 42.00% per annum, depending on card spends, credit limit utilization, repayment patterns, etc.
  • Cash advance fees: 2.5% of the billed amount withdrawn, minimum Rs. 500.
  • Late payment charges: Varies from nil to Rs. 1,300, depending on the due statement balance.
  • Over credit limit charge: 2.5% of the amount over the credit limit (excluding fees, charges and taxes), subject to a minimum of Rs. 500.
  • Returned payment charge: Rs. 500 per Cheque/ ECS/ ACH/ SI bounce.
  • Fuel transaction charge: 1% fuel transaction charge (or surcharge) + GST. This is reversed at authorized IndianOil Corporation outlets only for transactions above Rs. 10.
  • Railway ticket booking surcharge: 1.8% of the transaction value. 
  • Foreign currency transactions: 3.5% mark-up.
  • Cash deposit at Citi branches: Rs. 250 per deposit.

NOTE: Goods and Services Tax (GST) as notified by the Government of India is applicable on all the fees, interest and other charges and is subject to change

Citi Cash Back Credit Card Document Required

Following are the documents required to apply for the Citibank Cash Back card:

Documents required for salaried applicants

  • Identity proof: Valid Passport issued by the Government of India, Proof of Possession of Aadhaar number (via Aadhaar Card/ E-aadhaar), Voter's ID Card, Valid Driving License, Job Card issued by NREGA (duly signed by a State Government officer).
  • Address proof: Valid Passport issued by the Government of India, Proof of Possession of Aadhaar number (via Aadhaar Card/ E-aadhaar), Voter's ID Card, Valid Driving License, Job Card issued by NREGA (duly signed by a State Government officer).
  • Income proof: Latest 2 salary slips.

Documents required for non-salaried applicants

  • Identity proof: Valid Passport issued by the Government of India, Proof of Possession of Aadhaar number (via Aadhaar Card/ E-aadhaar), Voter's ID Card, Valid Driving License, Job Card issued by NREGA (duly signed by a State Government officer).
  • Address proof: Valid Passport issued by the Government of India, Proof of Possession of Aadhaar number (via Aadhaar Card/ E-aadhaar), Voter's ID Card, Valid Driving License, Job Card issued by NREGA (duly signed by a State Government officer).
  • Income proof: Latest year's Income Tax Return along with audited financials and computation of income.
