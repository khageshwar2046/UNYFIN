Axis Bank MY ZONE Credit Card Fees & Charges

Following are the details of Axis Bank MY ZONE credit card charges:

  • Joining fee: Nill
  • Annual fee: Nill
  • Joining and annual fees for Add-on card: Nil
  • Hotlisting charges: Nil
  • Duplicate statement fee/ Charge slip retrieval fee/ Copy request fee/ Outstation cheque fee/ Balance enquiry charges: Waived.
  • Cash payment fee: Rs. 100
  • Card replacement fee: Rs. 100
  • Finance charges for retail purchases & cash: 3.4% per month (49.36% p.a.)
  • Cash withdrawal fees: 2.5% (minimum Rs. 500) of the amount withdrawn
  • Overdue penalty/ Late payment fee: Up to Rs. 50,000 depending on the due payment amount.
  • Overlimit penalty: 2.5% of over the limit amount (minimum Rs. 500)
  • Cheque return/dishonour fee or auto-debit reversal: 2% of the amount (minimum Rs.450).
  • Foreign currency transaction fee: MY ZONE credit card charges 3.50% of the transaction value as forex charges.
  • Surcharge on purchase/cancellation of railway tickets: As mentioned by IRCTC/Indian Railways.
  • GST: As per existing Government norms.

Axis Bank MY ZONE Credit Card Eligibility

Following are the Axis Bank MY ZONE credit card eligibility criteria in detail:

  • Primary cardholder can be an Indian resident or a Non-Resident Indian (NRI). 
  • The Primary cardholder must be aged between 18 and 70 years. 
  • The Add-on cardholder should be above 15 years of age.
