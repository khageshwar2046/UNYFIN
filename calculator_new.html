    Loan against property

    Loan against property or LAP is a mortgage or secured loan availed after pledging a property as collateral. The interest rate starts from 7.25% p.a to 8.00% p.a. You can avail of LAP up to 70% of property value for maximum of 15 years.

    Home Loan

    Banks and other financial institutions offer home loans for the purchase of the property. You can also buy/construct/renovate/extend your house with this money and repay it gradually within the chosen time period. Home loans are secured against the property, and if you fail to repay, the lender can take over the property from you and sell it to recover the dues. The eligibility for a home loan depends on an applicant's income, credit history, property value and location.
