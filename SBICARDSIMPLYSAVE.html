Cashback & Rewards benefits

Every  100 spent from your SimplySAVE SBI Card earns you 1 Reward Point.

You can use earned points to pay the outstanding balance on your credit card or redeem against a wide array of gifts listed in the rewards catalogue.

Contactless Advantage

Avail contactless advantage with SimplySAVE SBI Card. The contactless technology enables transactions by merely waving the card. You do not need to swipe the card for transactions physically.

Fast & convenient transactions:  Wave the card and make transaction, no need to physically hand over card or bother about cash for small ticket everyday purchases.

Safe and Secure:  SimplySAVE SBI Card employs Visa payWave that ensures one transaction despite multiple card waves at the terminal.

The contactless technology also protects you against counterfeit transactions as card never leaves your hand.

Worldwide Acceptance

SimplySAVE SBI Card can be globally used at any outlet that accepts Visa or MasterCard. Precisely you can use your SBI Card over 24 million outlets, including 3,25,000 outlets in India.

Add-on Cards

You can apply for Add-On cards with SimplySAVE SBI Card for your parents, spouse, children or siblings. The Add On card must be applied for people above the age of 18.

Cash on the Go

You can conveniently withdraw cash from over 1 million Visa or MasterCard ATMs across the globe.

Utility Bill Payment Facility

With Easy Bill Pay facility you can instantly pay your utility bills for electricity, telephone or mobile.

Balance Transfer on EMI

Conveniently transfer the outstanding dues of your other credit cards to SimplySAVE SBI Card at a lower rate of interest and set affordable EMIs.

Flexipay

For any purchase of  2,500 or more on your SimplySAVE SBI Card you can opt for Flexipay and convert your outstanding balance into easy monthly instalments. To avail Flexipay facility log in with your username and password by visiting sbicard.com within 30 days of spending.

Easy Money

Easy Money on SBI Card ensures ready money for emergency. You can request a draft or cheque against your cash limit by logging in to sbicard.com. The cheque/draft is delivered at your doorstep.

  • Late payment: NIL for amount up to  200;  100 for dues ranging between  200 -  500;  400 for dues in the range of  500 -  1000;  600 for dues between  1000 -  10,000;  800 for dues from  10,000 -  25,000;  950 for dues greater than  25,000
  • Overlimit: 2.5% of the overlimit amount, minimum of  500
  • Card replacement:  100
  • Emergency card replacement, when abroad: Actual cost, with a minimum of $175
  • Foreign currency transaction: Conversion mark up - 3.5%
  • Dynamic Currency Conversion:
    • Mark up Fee: 3.50%

  • Priority Pass Lounge Charges: Under Priority Pass, all Airport Lounge visits within India will be charged with a usage fee of $27/ visit + applicable taxes.

  • All guests accompanied by the SBI Cardholder having the Priority Pass will be charged with a usage fee of $27/ visit + applicable taxes for visiting the Airport Lounge.
