Citi Prestige Credit Card Fees & Charges

  • Joining fee: Will be communicated at the time of sourcing.
  • Annual fee: Rs. 20,000 + taxes.
  • Cash advance fees: 2% of the billed amount withdrawn, minimum Rs. 300.
  • Late payment charges: Nil for statement balance up to Rs. 2000 and Rs. 100 for statement balance above Rs. 2,000.
  • Over credit limit charge: 2.5% of the amount over the credit limit (excluding fees, charges and taxes), subject to a minimum of Rs. 500.
  • Returned payment charge: Rs. 350 per Cheque/ ECS/ ACH/ SI bounce.
  • Railway ticket booking surcharge: 1.8% of the transaction value. 
  • Fuel transaction charge: 1% fuel transaction charge (or surcharge) + GST. This is reversed at authorized IndianOil Corporation outlets only for transactions above Rs. 10.
  • Foreign currency transactions: 3.5% mark-up.
  • Cash deposit at Citi branches: Rs. 100 per deposit.

Citi Prestige Credit Card Eligibility

You must meet the following eligibility criteria to apply for a Citi Prestige credit card:

  • You must be aged at least 21 years old or above to apply for the card.
  • Your minimum annual income should be at least Rs. 25 Lakhs.
  • You must be an Indian resident.
  • You should have a good credit history and credit score.

Citi Prestige Credit Card Document Required

Following are the documents your will be required to submit to apply for the Citi Prestige credit card:

Documents required for salaried applicants

  • Identity proof: Valid Passport issued by the Government of India, Proof of Possession of Aadhaar number (via Aadhaar Card/ E-aadhaar), Voter's ID Card, Valid Driving License, Job Card issued by NREGA (duly signed by a State Government officer).
  • Address proof: Valid Passport issued by the Government of India, Proof of Possession of Aadhaar number (via Aadhaar Card/ E-aadhaar), Voter's ID Card, Valid Driving License, Job Card issued by NREGA (duly signed by a State Government officer).
  • Income proof: Latest 2 salary slips.

Documents required for non-salaried applicants

  • Identity proof: Valid Passport issued by the Government of India, Proof of Possession of Aadhaar number (via Aadhaar Card/ E-aadhaar), Voter's ID Card, Valid Driving License, Job Card issued by NREGA (duly signed by a State Government officer).
  • Address proof: Valid Passport issued by the Government of India, Proof of Possession of Aadhaar number (via Aadhaar Card/ E-aadhaar), Voter's ID Card, Valid Driving License, Job Card issued by NREGA (duly signed by a State Government officer).
  • Income proof: Latest year's Income Tax Return along with audited financials and computation of income.

How to Apply for Citi Prestige Credit Card?

You can apply for a Citi Prestige credit card online through Citibank’s website or offline by visiting the nearby Citi branch. Alternatively, you can also apply for the card online on our portal by following the steps given below:

  • Click on the ‘Credit Cards’ tab available on the homepage of our website.
  • Enter all the necessary details such as your name, mobile number, email id, employment information, income, etc.
  • Verify your mobile number by typing in the OTP received on your number.
  • You will receive a call from one of our credit card experts soon to help you complete the credit card application process.
