Features of SimplyCLICK SBI Card

Welcome Gift:

As you join SimplyCLICK SBI Card, you get a welcome voucher from Amazon.in worth 500.

Terms & condition:  
It is a one-time welcome offer by the SBI Card and valid for 12 months from the day of joining. You become eligible for the offer only after the payment of the annual membership fee of 499. The Voucher cannot be redeemed in parts or in currency.

Reward Points:

As the name suggests, you earn on every simple click for online purchase via your SImplyCLICK SBI card. You get:

  • 10X reward points on e-spends with 6 exclusive partners*, i.e., Amazon, BookMyShow, Cleartrip, Foodpanda, Lenskart, and UrbanClap.
  • 5X rewards on all other online spends.
  • 100 spent online on all other websites, earn 1 to you.

Annual Fee & Joining Fee for SBI SimplyCLICK Credit Card

  • Joining fee: Rs. 499
  • Annual fee: Rs. 499 (Fee reversal on Rs. 1 Lakh spends in the previous year)

Eligibility for SBI SimplyCLICK Credit Card

  • Should be an Indian resident
  • Minimum age of 18 years
  • Must be salaried or self employed
  • Possess last 3 months bank statements

Documents required for SBI SimplyCLICK Credit Card

  • KYC fulfilment: Passport/ Voter's ID Card/ Driving Licence/ Aadhaar Letter or Card/NREGA Card/PAN Card
  • Address proof: Utility Bills/ Ration Card/ Passport
  • ID proof: Aadhar Card/ PAN card/ Driving license/ Voter Id Card
  • Income Proof: Last 3 Salary slips / bank statements
  • Photographs

How to avail the benefit of maximum reward points

  • The benefits and list of partner websites may change without prior notice. Kindly check the latest offers before availing the card.
  • You are eligible for rewards only when payment is made using SimplyCLICK SBI Card. Wallet transactions do not earn you 10X Reward Points.
  • Refer Rewards Catalogue to redeem your Credit Card Reward Points.
  • All other credit card facilities such as Cash Advance, Balance Transfer, Encash, Flexipay and Fuel transactions are not part of the Reward Points Program.

Benefits

Fuel Surcharge Waiver

With SimplyCLICK SBI Card you are eligible for 1% fuel surcharge waiver for each transaction between 500 & 3000 (exclusive of GST & all other applicable charges)

Contact less Advantage:    
Fast & convenient:

With SimplyCLICK SBI Card your daily purchases are convenient and fast. You enjoy contactless transactions, i.e., you need not hand over the card to make the purchase. Simply wave your card for everyday small purchases such as buying a ticket.

Security:

The contactless card transactions are safe and secure. No physical contact ensures lower chances of counterfeit transactions. The security key feature of Visa payWave ensures that only one transaction goes through despite multiple attempts during a transaction.

Global Acceptance

Your SimplyCLICK SBI Card is accepted at over 24 million outlets across the globe, including 3,25,000 outlets in India. Use your card at any outlet that accepts Visa or MasterCard.

Add-on Cards

With SimplyCLICK SBI Card, you can apply for add-on cards for your family, including your parents, spouse, children or siblings. The members should be above 18 years of age.

Cash on the Go

You can swipe your card to withdraw cash from over 1 million Visa or MasterCard ATMs across the globe.

Utility Bill Payment Facility

With Easy Bill Pay facility on your SimplyCLICK SBI Card, you can make hassle free payment of utility bills for electricity, telephone, mobile, etc.

Balance Transfer on EMI

Use Balance Transfer facility to save while paying your credit card dues. SimplyCLICK SBI Card offers a lower rate of interest on card EMIs.

Flexipay

With Flexipay, you can easily convert your transactions of  2,500 or above into easy EMIs within 30 days of making the purchase. You get complete freedom to choose the EMI plan according to your repayment capacity. The EMIs can be as low as  52 per  1,000.

Easy Money

With Easy Money facility, you can get doorstep delivery of a draft or cheque against your cash limit. All you need is to Log in with your username and password to sbicard.com to book for Easy Money facility.
