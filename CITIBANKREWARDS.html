Citi Rewards Credit Card Fees & Charges

The Citi Rewards credit card charges are mentioned below in detail:

  • Joining fee: Nil.
  • Annual & joining fee: Citi Rewards credit card annual fee is Rs. 1,000 + GST. The fee is waived off on spending Rs. 30,000 or more on the card. 
  • Cash advance fee: 2.5% on the billed withdrawn amount, minimum Rs. 500.
  • Late payment charges: Up to Rs. 1,300.
  • Over credit limit charge: 2.5% of the amount over the credit limit, minimum Rs. 500. 
  • Returned payment charge: Rs. 500 per returned Cheque/ ECS/ ACH/ SI bounce payment.
  • Fuel transaction charge: 1% of fuel transaction charge (or surcharge) + GST. Reversed at authorized IndianOil Corporation outlets for transactions above Rs. 10.
  • Railway ticket booking surcharge: 1.8% of the transaction value.
  • Reissuance of lost/ stolen/ damaged card: Nil.
  • Foreign currency transaction: Citibank credit card charges foreign currency transaction charges of 3.5%. 
  • Cash deposit at Citi branches: Rs. 250 per deposit.

Features & Benefits of Citi Rewards Credit Card

Following are the Citi Rewards credit card benefits and features:

Welcome Gift

  • Get up to 2500 welcome reward points as a welcome gift with your Citibank Rewards card.

Accelerated Rewards

  • Citibank Rewards card offers 10 reward points on spending every Rs.125 on apparel and department stores (online and in-store).
  • Get 1 reward point on spending Rs. 125 on all other spends on the Citi Rewards card.

Bonus Rewards

  • Get 300 bonus points on the purchase of Rs. 30,000 or more in a month with Citi bank Rewards credit card.

Evergreen Reward Points

  • Reward points earned with your Citibank Rewards card never expire. You can redeem them whenever you wish to.
  • Citibank reward points can be redeemed instantly via SMS against purchases at leading brands.

Citi FlexiBill

Convert your credit card outstanding balance into 6 to 48 instalments with Citi FlexiBill.

  • The minimum amount should be Rs. 2,500 for availing this Citibank Rewards card benefits.
  • EMI payment options start from as low as Rs. 74 per month.

Citi Rewards Credit Card Eligibility

You have to meet the following eligibility criteria to apply for a Citi Rewards card:

  • The applicant should be aged between 18 to 70 years.
  • The applicant must be a resident of India.

Citi Rewards Credit Card Document Required

Following is the Citi Reward card documents requirement: 

Documents required for salaried applicants

  • Identity proof: Valid Passport issued by Government of India, Driving License, Aadhaar Card/ E-aadhaar, Voter's ID Card, or Job Card issued by NREGA (duly signed by an officer of the State Government).
  • Address proof: Valid Passport issued by the Government of India, Driving License, Aadhaar Card/ E-aadhaar, Voter's ID Card, or Job Card issued by NREGA (duly signed by an officer of the State Government).
  • Income proof: Latest 2 salary slips.

Documents required for self-employed applicants

  • Identity proof: Valid Passport issued by Government of India, Driving License, Aadhaar Card/ E-aadhaar, or Voter's ID Card.
  • Address proof: Valid Passport issued by the Government of India, Driving License, Aadhaar Card/ E-aadhaar, or Voter's ID Card. 
  • Income proof: Latest year ITR with audited financials & computation of income.
